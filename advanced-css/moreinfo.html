My Kuromi Collection

I stated on a previous page that I collect Kuromi, a Sanrio character, figures. So here's my collection! Please excuse the dust and awkward angles, I was trying to fit into the fewest pictures possible. Some are not picture due to still being in the boxes and I also have a flail tattoo of her, the ball being her head.

Where I Find my Figures

I find Kuromi's at many places, trying not to order offline. Walmart (on rare occasions), Five Below, Miniso (on the 2nd floor of Hamilton Place mall), my mom got me a few at the flea market, and a couple other places.
